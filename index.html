Lightweight Cross-Lingual Speaker Adaptation
for Indic TTS

Audio samples accompanying the paper

We propose a lightweight speaker adaptation pipeline requiring only a 10-second reference sample. Synthetic speech from IN-F5 is filtered through a four-stage quality-control pipeline and used to fine-tune a FastSpeech2 model with dual-site ECAPA-TDNN speaker conditioning and a cosine consistency loss. CLS-based phoneme unification enables the adapted voice to synthesise speech across Hindi, Marathi, Tamil, and Telugu — running 53× faster than IN-F5 at 4.7× lower parameter count.
